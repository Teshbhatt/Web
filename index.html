Game Rules

  1. Set up a physical chess board with the printed QR codes on each square.
  2. Players take turns scanning a QR code where they want to move their piece.
  3. Upon scanning, a Python loops question appears on the screen.
  4. Answer the question correctly to move your piece to that square.
  5. Answer incorrectly and you must try again with another question.
  6. Normal chess rules apply - first player to checkmate wins!
  7. Your score is tracked based on correct answers and game completion.
